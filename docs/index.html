The problem

Recovering focus
is the real villain.

The interruption isn't the issue. It's what happens after — the 10 minutes it takes to remember where you were, what was next, and what you were just thinking. That's the cost.

Floaty attacks it from two angles.

01

Never lose the thread

Your current task and next event float above every app, on every screen. No matter what pulls you away, one glance and you're back — exactly where you left off.

02

Capture the idea, keep the flow

Something hits you mid-meeting? Hit +, dump it into Google Tasks in 2 seconds, and get back. The thought is safe. Your focus stays intact. Nothing slips through.

Privacy

Your data never leaves your Mac.

Floaty has no backend. Your tasks and calendar data go directly from Google to your screen — full stop.

Read the privacy policy →
🔑
Tokens in Keychain
Your Google login is stored in macOS Keychain. Encrypted, local, yours.
🚫
No third-party servers
Your Mac talks directly to Google. Nothing passes through anyone else.
👁
Fully open source
Every line of code is on GitHub. Read exactly what runs on your Mac.
📋
Read-only by default
Floaty only reads your Calendar and Tasks. It writes back only when you check a task off.
