Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Floaty is tiny, focused, and gets out of your way.

📌

Always on top

Floats above every app, every window, every Space. Your calendar is always one glance away.

✅

One-click check-off

Click the circle to mark a task done. Confetti. Voice. Back to work.

🔄

Live sync

Refreshes every minute. Current event, next event, or your task list — always up to date.

✏️

Add tasks instantly

Hit + to add a task to Google Tasks without switching apps or breaking focus.

Drag anywhere

Auto-updates

Setup

Running in
3 minutes

No Terminal. No configuration. Just download and go.

1

Download & install

Download the DMG, open it, drag Floaty to Applications. That's the whole install.

2

Sign in with Google

Your browser opens for a standard Google sign-in. Authorize Calendar and Tasks access.

3

Floaty appears

The widget shows up. Drag it to your favorite corner and get back to work.
