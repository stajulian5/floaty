Setup

Running in
3 minutes.

No Terminal. No config files. Just download and go.

1

Download & install

Open the DMG and drag Floaty to Applications. Classic Mac install — done in 10 seconds.

2

Sign in with Google

Your browser opens for a standard Google sign-in. Tap Allow for Calendar and Tasks. That's it.

3

You're back in the game

Floaty appears. Drag it to your favorite corner. Never lose your place again.
