Setup

Up and running
in 3 minutes

One command handles everything.

1

Run the install command

Paste curl -fsSL …/install.sh | bash in Terminal. It installs dependencies and sets Floaty to launch at login.

2

Sign in with Google

Your browser opens to Google's authorization page. Grant Calendar and Tasks access — that's it.

3

Floaty appears

The widget shows up on your screen. Drag it to your preferred spot and get back to work.

🔒 Your data stays on your Mac

Floaty talks directly to Google's APIs from your computer. No data ever passes through a third-party server. Tokens are stored in your macOS Keychain. Privacy policy →
